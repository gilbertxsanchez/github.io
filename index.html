About Me

My name is Gilbert and I am a business analytics professional most passionate about problem-solving, creativity, and interpersonal connection.

I began my career as a customer-facing operations professional and quickly transitioned my career into data analytics at the start of the COVID-19 lockdown. Since then, I have been able to join both experiences that provide a unique firsthand perspective to data-driven business insights.

I love getting to know new people and having engaging conversations about whatever you are passionate about. If you have any questions or want to connect, please feel free to reach out!.

Problem Solver

I am able to provide end-to-end data analysis that ranges from data gathering and cleaning all the way to executive-level presentation in order to communicate business recommendations.

Some of the skills and tools that I use to achieve this are:

  • Spreadsheets (Google Sheets, MS Excel)
  • SQL Relational Databases (Snowflake, AWS Redshift, MS SQL Server)
  • BI & Visualization Tools (Looker, Tableau, Power BI, Amplitude, Domo)
  • Coding Languages (Python, JavaScript)
  • Statistical testing ( Correlation, T-test, ANOVA)

Amateur Creative

I find enjoyment in expanding my creative horizons by trying new tools and mediums that I have never been exposed to in the past. I believe that art and creativity not only increase the quality of life but also can also expand different avenues of approaching real-world problems.

Some of the skills and tools I use creatively are:

  • Digital Audio Workstations (Audacity, Audition, Reaper)
  • Digital Illustration/Graphic Design (Krita, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, GIMP)
  • Video Editing (Shotcut, Adobe Premier, DaVinci Resolve)
  • Social Media (YouTube, Discord, Instagram, Spotify for Podcasters, Twitch)

Open Door
